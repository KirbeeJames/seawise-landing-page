Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

1. Introduction

JK Yacht Ops ("we", "us", "our") operates SeaWise, a maritime distance, fuel consumption, travel time and cost calculator available as a web application and mobile app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

When you create an account and use SeaWise, we may collect the following information:

  • Account information: Email address and password (used for authentication)
  • Vessel specifications: Vessel name, fuel capacity, fuel curve data (RPM, speed, burn rate)
  • Route data: Origin and destination ports, generated waypoints, route calculations
  • Trip history: Saved voyage records including distance, fuel consumption, cost estimates, and dates
  • Fuel market data: Fuel prices you track or sync for specific ports

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate the SeaWise service
  • Calculate port-to-port distances, fuel consumption, travel times, and voyage costs
  • Generate AI-powered maritime route suggestions
  • Sync your data across devices via cloud storage
  • Improve and develop new features for the service

4. AI Processing Disclosure

SeaWise uses Google Gemini AI to generate maritime route waypoints and provide port coordinate lookups. When you request a route or port lookup:

  • Only port names and geographic coordinates are sent to the AI service
  • No personal information, account data, or vessel specifications are transmitted to Google
  • AI requests are processed via a secure serverless proxy and are not stored by Google for training purposes

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely in a Supabase cloud database with the following protections:

  • Row-level security (RLS) ensures you can only access your own data
  • All data is encrypted in transit via HTTPS/TLS
  • Authentication is handled by Supabase Auth with secure password hashing
  • We do not store your password in plain text

6. Third-Party Services

SeaWise uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Google Gemini API: AI-powered route generation and port lookups
  • Supabase: User authentication and cloud database
  • Vercel: Web application hosting and serverless API functions

We do not use any advertising networks. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in SeaWise
  • Correct any inaccurate information in your account or vessel profiles
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Export your trip history and vessel data

To exercise these rights, you can manage your data directly within the application or contact us at the email below.

8. Guest Access

SeaWise offers guest access without account creation. Guest users can use the calculator and route planning features, but their data is stored locally in the browser only and is not synced to the cloud. No personal information is collected from guest users.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
